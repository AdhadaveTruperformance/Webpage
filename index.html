About Us

CRIMINAL DEFENCE

San Mateo & Santa Clara Countries

The Law Office of Thomas Greenberg is dedicated to handling criminal defense matters. We work with a team of investigators, toxicologists, and scientific experts.

Attorney Thomas Greenberg is passionate about criminal defense. His extensive knowledge and experience give him the edge you need to properly handle your case.

Thomas Greenberg is a native of California and a graduate of the University of California at Berkeley. He was formerly a highly respected deputy public defender and is now an experienced San Mateo County criminal defense attorney who has provided effective legal services to thousands of clients.

His cases have ranged from DUIs to three strikes from arraignment through jury trial. This experience has provided him with a thorough understanding of how prosecutors think, how they prepare their evidence for trial, and how he can best protect your rights.

He has earned a reputation as one of the toughest and hardest-hitting defense lawyers in California. He has defended criminal cases for professional athletes, police officers, and even criminal defense lawyers.

Mr. Greenberg handles his clients' cases personally.
